A letter for Meenakshi Seth

Dear Mumma,

Happy Birthday. On your special day, I wanted to make something that could stay with you — a small place made with love, where my gratitude for you could be written properly and where you could always come back and feel how deeply you are loved.

Thank you for everything you have done for me and for our family. A mother gives in ways the world does not always see. She sacrifices comfort, time, rest, and peace of mind so that her family can feel safe, supported, and loved. You have done that with strength and grace, again and again, and I will never take that for granted.

You have been my guide when I did not know which direction to take, my safe space, and my source of strength when things became difficult. So much of who I am has been shaped by your love, your values, and the way you have always stood by me.

You mean so much not only to me, but to our whole family. Your presence brings warmth into our home, your care keeps people together, and your love leaves a deep mark on everyone lucky enough to receive it. You make life better simply by being in it.

On your birthday, I pray that life gives you back the same goodness you have given others. I wish you health that stays strong, happiness that grows every year, peace in your heart, beautiful moments with family, and a long life filled with love, dignity, and joy.

Thank you for being you, for loving so selflessly, and for being one of the greatest blessings of my life.

With all my love,
Divyansh ❤️
